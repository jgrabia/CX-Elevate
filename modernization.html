Our modernization playbook

A structured, low-risk path inspired by proven enterprise patterns—incremental delivery, not a multi-year rewrite.

Incremental modernization

Small, reviewable changes—replace deprecated APIs, extract modules, remove dead code—shipped in tight PRs with fast feedback.

Risk-based prioritization

We tackle low-risk, high-impact areas first. Complex or business-critical paths get extra testing and staged rollouts.

Test-driven refactoring

Baseline tests before structural changes. AI helps generate and extend coverage; CI gates block regressions.

Domain-driven decomposition

Identify bounded contexts and peel off services over time—strangler-fig style—so you modernize without stopping the business.
